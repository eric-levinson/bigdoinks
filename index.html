Mining Instructions:

  1. Create an electroneum wallet at electroneum.com.
  2. Copy your Public Wallet Address key. (This should start with 'etn')
  3. Paste your Public Wallet Address key into the 'Mining Key' textbox in the Config section.
  4. Click / Press the 'Start' button one time. (do not hit your enter key, do not hit start twice)
  5. Youre Done! (if your public mining key is good the miner should start to hash.
